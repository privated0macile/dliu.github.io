My Interests

One of my main academic interests is financial markets and how data can be used to analyze them. Markets are influenced by economic growth, interest rates, policy decisions, and investor behavior. Understanding these forces requires both analytical thinking and the ability to interpret data.

Another area that interests me is data visualization. Visualization tools allow analysts to transform large datasets into intuitive graphics that reveal trends and patterns. Charts and dashboards can help communicate complex financial information in a clear and accessible way.

Combining finance with programming and data science allows analysts to explore markets using quantitative techniques. Through studying finance and working with data tools, I hope to develop skills that help me analyze markets and investment opportunities more effectively.

Topics I Enjoy Learning About

  • Equity markets and investing
  • Macroeconomic trends
  • Financial modeling
  • Data visualization
  • Programming with Python
