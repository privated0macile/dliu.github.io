Academic Interests

My primary academic interests are centered around financial markets and the role data plays in understanding economic systems. Financial markets are influenced by numerous factors including macroeconomic conditions, interest rates, and investor expectations.

Data analysis and visualization are increasingly important tools for interpreting financial information. By transforming complex datasets into clear visual insights, analysts are able to better understand patterns and trends that influence investment decisions.

Combining finance with data science allows analysts to develop quantitative approaches to understanding markets. Through programming, statistical analysis, and financial modeling, it becomes possible to evaluate risk, forecast trends, and analyze investment opportunities more effectively.

  • Macroeconomic analysis
  • Financial modeling
  • Data visualization
