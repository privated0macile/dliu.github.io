My Academic Interests

One of the topics I enjoy studying most is finance and how markets behave over time. Financial markets are influenced by a wide range of factors including economic growth, interest rates, government policy, and investor sentiment. Understanding these forces requires both analytical thinking and the ability to interpret large amounts of data.

Another area that interests me is data visualization. Data visualization helps transform raw numbers into visual insights that are easier for people to understand. Charts, graphs, and dashboards allow analysts to identify patterns and trends that might otherwise be difficult to notice in large datasets.

Combining finance and data science is especially exciting because it allows us to analyze markets more effectively. By using programming tools and statistical models, analysts can evaluate risk, forecast trends, and build investment strategies based on quantitative evidence rather than intuition alone.

Topics I Enjoy Learning About

  • Financial markets and investing
  • Macroeconomic trends
  • Data visualization
  • Programming with Python
  • Investment strategies
